How does it work?

Give+Take is more than just a shop - we operate a part-exchange system and take donations too.

Give + Take - Exchange

If you have clean and desirable clothing languishing at the back of a wardrobe, or pieces of furniture you don't really have room for, bring them in to Give+Take! We'll give you store credit of 50% of your items selling price.

Give - Donations

Of course, you can donate your items too - if you do, 100% of the profits will go to Breakthrough, our supported charity.

Take - New items

Everything else we sell is brand new, and we try to responsibly source all of our stock. We add a 1 discretionary donation to Breakthrough on all purchases over 10.

Charities

Give+Take supports Breakthrough Breast Cancer because three-quarters of their spending goes into research and they have no stores of their own.

Breakthrough is a pioneering charity dedicated to the prevention, treatment and ultimate eradication of breast cancer.

100% of the profit from Donations goes to Breakthrough, as well as the 1 discretionary charge we add to all other purchases over 10.

Through our choice of suppliers for new products, we are supporting a number of other charities around the world.

Frames

Recycled timber picture frames from South Africa. Homeless and indigent people in and around Cape Town earn a sustainable income from collecting the timber.

Pots

Kew Gardens receives a donation from each pot sold.

Glasses

All of our glasses are made from recycled glass.

Beauty

"All For Eve" has been set up with the sole intention to raise awareness and funds for gynaecological cancer research by donating all net profits to The Eve Appeal. When you buy "all for eve" products you are choosing to make a difference to the lives of all women across the UK.
